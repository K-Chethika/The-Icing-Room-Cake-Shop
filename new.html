About The Icing Room

Our Story

The Icing Room was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to create the most delicious, beautiful cakes using only the finest ingredients. What started as a small home-based bakery has grown into one of Colombo's most beloved cake shops.

Our founder, Anjali Perera, learned the art of baking from her grandmother, who owned a small bakery in Kandy. With traditional recipes and modern techniques, we create cakes that are both visually stunning and incredibly delicious.

We believe that every celebration deserves a special cake, whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or just a Tuesday night. That's why we pour our hearts into every cake we create, ensuring it's perfect for your special moment.
